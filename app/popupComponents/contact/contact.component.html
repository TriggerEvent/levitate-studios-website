Frequent Questions

Frequently Asked Questions:

+-  What do I need to play Turbo Squad?

Turbo squad can be played on IOS and Android on recommended devices. The game is designed for both mobile and tablet.

+-  Can I download Turbo Squad from your website?

Yes! You can visit the store and download directly to your device. To visit the store, use any of the links on our home page or click here for android and here for IOS.

+-  Can I delete my game or recover lost progress?

To delete your game you must uninstall Turbo Squad from your device, when re-installed you will be prompted to sync to the cloud or download previous progress. If you sync, all previous progress will be lost and/or updated. If you download, your last cloud sync will be recovered.

+-  Will my progress be saved across all my devices?

If you are logged in and progress has been saved to the cloud, you will be able to sync this progress across your devices.

+-  Can I save progress without using the cloud (offline)?

Yes, all data will be stored directly to your phone if you choose to play offline or do not wish to use the cloud.
